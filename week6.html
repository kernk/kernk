Fusion 360

I created a sticker using the printer by giving it a special pink outline that the machine reads to cut the sides of the sticker and a different sticker by tracing the outline and then using the printer to cut out the details and then pick out the empty parts of design before putting it onto transfer paper and then onto my notebook. | the mold of my head was then filled with latex and the latex was filled with chocolate to make chocolate the shape of my head.
