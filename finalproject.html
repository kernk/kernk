Computer-Aided Design

Week 9 | final project

I made my final project with Mirriam.

Fusion 360

For my final project we created a box, we used fusion 360 to design our square shaped box before 3d printing it, next we used correll draw to design a star pattern which we laser cutted into the wood on the raster setting so it did not go through the wood, we put wood on the top and bottom . After that we stained the wood a natural color and then we attatched them with nails and screws. This took about 2 and a half class periods. I learned how to use the 3D printer and laser cutter better throughout this process.
